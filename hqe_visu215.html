HQE VISU2.1.5 – Access to Views

Visual Comfort indicator · Référentiels HQE-B v1.1 / HQE-BD v4.1 · CERTIVEA April 2025

Urban Context — applies to all rooms
The urban context determines which view components (sky, landscape, ground) are expected for each floor level. It is a property of the building's surroundings, not of individual rooms.
